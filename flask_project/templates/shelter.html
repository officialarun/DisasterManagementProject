Shelter Guidelines

  • Always adhere to the guidelines provided by shelter authorities and staff.
  • Bring necessary items such as medications, important documents, personal identification, etc
  • Adhere to health protocols.
  • If you notice any safety concerns, health issues, or conflicts, report them to shelter staff immediately.
  • Keep your belongings packed and be ready to move quickly if instructed by authorities to evacuate the shelter.
